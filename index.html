SUSTAINABLE FASHION

By remedying the practices of the current fashion industry, we can create a more sustainable and environmentally-friendly future that is built to last.

Flying under the radar to most, the fashion industry is a notable contributor to greenhouse gas emissions and microplastic losses to the ocean. In addition, the amount of fabric waste each year, through both overproduction or disposal from consumers is astronomical. Many of these fabrics end up in landfills instead of being recycled, and in some cases they are incinerated. The industry as a whole must change its practices if it wishes to remain stable, and we as consumers must also reframe our mindset to bring about that change.
