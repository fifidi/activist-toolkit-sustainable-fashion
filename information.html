INFORMATION

Fast Facts:

The clothing and textile industry is responsible for 2-8% of global greenhouse gas emissions, mainly through the factory production system.

Textiles, especially plastic-based fabrics such as polyester, account for 9% of microplastic losses to the ocean.

On a yearly basis, the United States throws away 11.3 million tons of fabric waste.

The UN Alliance for Sustainable Fashion

The United Nations created the Alliance for Sustainable Fashion in coordination with their Sustainable Development Goals, which is a set of 17 goals that they wish to achieve by 2030. This site is a useful resource for a more global look at the textile industry.

Zara JOIN LIFE Sustainablility Innovation Hub

Zara is known to be one of the major trendsetters of the fashion industry. Within their sustainability efforts, and their JOIN LIFE collection, they work with different smaller companies, most recently Renewcell, who help to create more responsibly-made pieces.
