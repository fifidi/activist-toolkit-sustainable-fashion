TAKE ACTION

1

Buy less clothing. Try a challenge, such as Project 333, where you only wear 33 pieces of clothing for 3 months!

2

Buy sustainably made clothing. Try to avoid plastic-based fabrics and buy clothes with recycled content.

3

Wear your clothes for as long as possible, and when their life is over, donate them or ensure that they are recycled!

These are a few basic steps you, the consumer, can take to work towards a more sustainable fashion industry. There are so many other things that you can do. Keep innovating and finding solutions to our world's problems!
